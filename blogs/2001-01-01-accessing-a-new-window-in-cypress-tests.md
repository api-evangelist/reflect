Introduction When you find yourself in the process of developing a more complex web page, you’ll surely come across a situation where the users click on buttons/links that will take them to a new tab or window. While it is straightforward to code such a button/link, testing this behavior can be quite a bit more difficult. Cypress, for instance, doesn’t have native support for accessing a new window or browser tab.
